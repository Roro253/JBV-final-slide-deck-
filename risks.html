Investment Risks and Mitigation

Key Insight: While AI investments offer exceptional upside potential, they also carry specific risks that require strategic mitigation. Our approach balances risk management with maintaining exposure to category-defining opportunities.

Valuation Risk
Current AI company valuations may be elevated relative to traditional metrics, creating potential for valuation corrections.
Mitigation: Staged investment approach with tranched capital deployment; focus on companies with clear path to profitability.
Regulatory Risk
Evolving regulatory landscape for AI could impact business models, slow adoption, or increase compliance costs.
Mitigation: Diversification across jurisdictions; focus on companies with robust compliance frameworks and ethical AI principles.
Technology Risk
Rapid technological change could render current AI approaches obsolete or create new competitive threats.
Mitigation: Portfolio diversification across AI stack layers; invest in companies with strong R&D capabilities and technical talent.
Market Timing Risk
AI adoption curves may progress differently than projected, affecting growth rates and return timelines.
Mitigation: Balanced portfolio across early and growth-stage companies; maintain liquidity reserves for extended investment horizons.
